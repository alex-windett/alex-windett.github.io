About Me

Starting off as a Full Stack developer in 2014 after learning web development at General Assembly, I now focus my skills as a Frontend Developer and UX Designer.

Currently working at Make Us Proud in London, I am building and designing the Beta version of Joyent's upgrade to its cloud container service. Prior to this, I was given the role of lead dev on a number of WordPress builds including 9Bar and Tyrrells', later on leading a small agile team to create a Single Page Web app with React / Redux for Freddie's Flowers.

I am always interested in developing my skills further, and use my knowledge as a Frontend Developer to improve the UX process. Outside of work I try and fit attending meetups and conferences around a semi-hectic cycling schedule - competing in road races, a few track events and the odd end of season triathlon to name a few.

About Me

Starting off as a Full Stack developer in 2014 after learning web development at General Assembly, I now focus my skills as a Front End Devloper and also contribute in with the UX design process.

Currently working as a UX Designer and Front End Developer at Make Us Proud in London, I am designing and creating an initial prototype for a competitor to AWS. Previously, I was given the role of lead dev on a number of WordPress builds including 9Bar and Tyrrells', later on leading a small agile team to create a Single Page Web app with React / Redux for Freddie's Flowers.

I am always interested in developing my skills further, and use my knowledge as a Front End Developer to improve the Ux process. Outside of work I try and fit attending meetups and conferences around a semi-hectic cycling schedule - competing in road races, a few track events and the odd triathlon end of the season to name a few.
